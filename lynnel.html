About me

Part scientist,
part chef

Hi, I'm Amara — a food science & nutrition student with a deep fascination for what happens between the farm and the fork. I work at the intersection of microbiology, food chemistry, and product development, driven by questions like: how do we make fermented foods safer, more nutritious, and more accessible to East African households?

When I'm not in the lab, I'm testing recipes, reading food policy, or thinking about how science can address food security at scale.
